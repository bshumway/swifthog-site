LokStok keeps your firearm records private and organized

A secure, local-only vault for inventory, maintenance logs, ammo tracking, documents, and reports. No accounts, no cloud sync, no analytics.

Visit Help Center Contact Support

What you can do in LokStok

Secure firearm inventory

Store firearm details and keep records encrypted on-device with SQLCipher.

Maintenance tracking

Log cleanings and monitor maintenance status as your usage changes.

Ammo and session logs

Track ammo inventory and connect usage with shooting sessions.

Documents and NFA records

Keep files and important records organized in one private vault.

Reports and exports

Generate report outputs for insurance, records, and personal organization.

Backup and restore

Create encrypted backups and restore data when moving devices.
