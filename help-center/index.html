Quick start

  1. Complete onboarding and set your PIN.
  2. Enable biometrics for faster secure unlock.
  3. Add your first firearm and key details.
  4. Log ammo and maintenance as you use your equipment.
  5. Create an encrypted backup and store it in a safe location.

Core features

Inventory management

Track firearm records and related details in one organized vault.

Maintenance logs

Record cleanings and monitor status over time.

Ammo tracking

Manage ammo inventory and log usage associated with sessions.

NFA and documents

Store and organize documents and records tied to your items.

Reports and exports

Generate useful outputs for personal records and planning.

Global search

Find records quickly across your vault content.

Security and privacy

  • Local-only data model with no app-operated cloud sync.
  • Encrypted database at rest with key protection on device.
  • Biometric unlock with PIN fallback.
  • No analytics tracking and no advertising SDKs.

Backup and recovery

  • Create encrypted backups regularly from Settings.
  • Store backup files in a trusted location you control.
  • Test your restore flow occasionally to confirm recoverability.
  • Keep backup password details secure and memorable.

Troubleshooting checklist

  • Update to the latest app version.
  • Restart your device if biometric prompts fail to appear.
  • Verify device storage space if imports/exports fail.
  • Contact support with app version and device OS details.
