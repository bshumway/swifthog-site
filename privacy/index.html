Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how SwiftHog ("we", "our", "us") handles information when you use Firearms Vault.

1) Data handling approach

Firearms Vault is designed as a local-first app. Data is stored on your device and is encrypted at rest. We do not require account creation to use core features.

2) Information we collect

We do not collect personal information directly through the app. We do not operate analytics, ad tracking, or cloud data collection in the app.

3) Information you store in the app

You may choose to store firearm and inventory records in the app. This content remains under your control on your device.

4) Data sharing

We do not sell personal information and do not share in-app stored data with third parties through app-operated servers.

5) Security

We use technical safeguards such as local encryption and device-protected storage. However, no software can guarantee absolute security.

6) Your choices

You can remove app data by deleting records within the app or uninstalling the app, subject to your device backup settings.

7) Children

Firearms Vault is not directed to children under 18, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8) Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates are posted on this page with a revised effective date.

9) Contact

For privacy questions, contact support@swifthog.com.

Note: review this policy with counsel to ensure it matches your legal obligations in your jurisdictions.
